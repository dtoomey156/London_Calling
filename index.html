London Calling Fan Page

Fan page for the chart topping, platinum selling, third album by The Clash


originally released as a double album in the United Kingdom on 14 December 1979 by CBS Records, and in the United States in January 1980 by Epic Records, London Calling marks a thematic a stylistic turning point for The Clash, bridging the traditional punk rock sound with an emerging post-punk aesthetic. London Calling reflects the bands growing interest in styles beyond punk roots, including reggae, rockabilly, ska, New Orleans R&B, pop, lounge jazz and hard rock, and introduces themes of social displacement, unemployment, racial conflict, drug abuse, and the responsibilities of adulthood.
